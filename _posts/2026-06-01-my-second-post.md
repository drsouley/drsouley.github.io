## Learning more every day!

This is my second post. Notice how:
- I didn't edit the homepage at all
- The "Recent Posts" section updated automatically
- The date format is consistent

### That's the magic of Jekyll!

Every time I add a file to `_posts/`, Jekyll:
1. Turns it into a webpage
2. Adds it to the recent posts list
3. Creates a permalink automatically
