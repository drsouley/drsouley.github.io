# Design Look & Feel: GRC & AI Insights

This design system, **Governance & Intelligence System**, is tailored for a professional technical journal at the intersection of regulatory frameworks and machine learning.

## Visual Identity
The aesthetic is rooted in **minimalist authority**. By utilizing a strictly text-centric approach, the design prioritizes the content's integrity and the reader's focus.

- **Color Palette:** A sophisticated scale of slate and navy (`#0f172a`) against a clean, off-white surface (`#f7f9fb`). This high-contrast pairing ensures maximum readability and a "printed journal" feel.
- **Typography:** The use of **Geist** provides a modern, engineered feel. Bold, tight-tracked headings command attention, while spacious body text with a 1.6 line-height ensures a comfortable long-form reading experience.
- **Layout:** A grid-based system with generous section gaps (`80px`) and desktop margins (`64px`). The "Article Template" uses a centered, single-column measure to prevent eye fatigue.

## Key Design Patterns
- **Text-Only Content:** Complete removal of imagery, headshots, and thumbnails to reinforce a serious, data-driven persona.
- **Micro-Interactions:** Subtle hover states on navigation and list items using `bg-surface-container-low` to provide feedback without breaking the minimalist aesthetic.
- **Borders & Rules:** Hairline strokes (`1px`) in `outline-variant` are used sparingly to define hierarchy and separate content blocks without adding visual weight.
